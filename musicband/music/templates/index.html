About Us

Entro is a vibrant Malayalam music band known for blending traditional Kerala sounds with modern musical genres, creating a unique and captivating sonic experience. Drawing from the rich heritage of Kerala's folk and classical music, Entro fuses elements like Carnatic vocals, earthy percussion, and instruments like chenda and edakka with contemporary rock, jazz, and electronic influences. The band’s lyrics often explore themes of nature, social issues, and cultural pride, resonating deeply with the Malayalam-speaking audience.

Entro’s live performances are dynamic, marked by high-energy rhythms and a sense of nostalgia, enhanced by visuals that reflect the beauty and spirit of Kerala. This has helped the band build a strong local fan base, while their progressive approach attracts listeners from across India and beyond.
